Mars is a popular chocolate bar produced by the American company Mars Inc.

The Mars as falling outside the United States is known, is a 9 inches long band of circa. 51 grams. It consists of a substance of a floury chocolate, covered with caramel and covered with milk chocolate (solid). The energy value is high: the wrapper mentions 1021 kilojoule. The packaging is black with the brand in red letters; years of marketing has made a strong and recognizable brand of this appearance.

Did You Know?

The Mars bar was created in 1932 by Forrest Mars, Sr. in the United States. It quickly became one of the most popular chocolate bars worldwide, thanks to its combination of nougat, caramel, and milk chocolate. Over the years, the Mars bar has inspired countless variations and has remained a favorite treat for chocolate lovers of all ages!
